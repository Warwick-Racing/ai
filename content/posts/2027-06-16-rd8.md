## RD8

What Does RD8 Actually Do?

RD8 builds tolerance stack-up analysis and optimisation software for engineering teams — and it's genuinely impressive stuff. At its core, the platform helps engineers understand and control how manufacturing tolerances accumulate across an assembly, ensuring that parts fit together and function correctly even when each individual component sits at the edge of its allowed variation.

What sets RD8 apart is their excellent analysis software which scans a 3D CAD model and automatically identifies mechanical interfaces and overconstraints. Rather than spending days building tolerance stacks manually in spreadsheets, engineers can import their model let RD8 detect and set up tolerance chains in minutes — with built-in sanity checks to catch errors before they become expensive problems downstream.

In short: RD8 helps engineering teams ship flawless designs the first time, reducing design loops, cutting part costs, and building confidence from day one of development. It's exactly the kind of forward-thinking planning that resonates with what we're trying to achieve here at WRAI — and we're thrilled to have them in our corner. 🙏
