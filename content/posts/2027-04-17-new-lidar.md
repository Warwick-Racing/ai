## We've Upgraded Our Lidar — and It's a Big Deal

The AI team has just moved from our trusty Hesai XT32 to the brand new **Hesai ATX** — and honestly, it's not even a close comparison. This thing is in a completely different league.

## Seeing Further Than Ever Before

The most immediately exciting observation is just how far this expands our cone perception. Although the XT32 could return points out to around **120 m** in reality the small size of the cones and the beam spread of the lidar meant that detection became tricky at **~20 m** as the cones literally disappear between adjacent laser rings.

With the ATX pushes that to **230 m at 10% reflectivity** for points, but more importantly with vastly more channels in a smaller vertical FoV we are get a much denser cloud to work with and so the cones remain obvious in the cloud at **70 m** and counting (we ran out of room).

Full formal testing is still on the todo list until we update the perception pipeline to the new sensor, but it is clear that the ATX is going to significantly improve our detection range capabilities.
